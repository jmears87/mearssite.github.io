Joe Mears's AENG 110 Print Project

This page is dedicated to the lithographic printing I did in Applied Engineering 110 this semester.

With this project, we were expected to design a bookmark with 4 other people who are all designing their own bookmark. then we create a digital layout of all 5. once we have that layout, we use the lithographic printers to print about 50 copies. We then use a large cutting machine to trim out bookmarks individually.
